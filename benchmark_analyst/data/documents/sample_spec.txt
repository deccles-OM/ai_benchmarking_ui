FORD F150 VEHICLE SPECIFICATION & WARRANTY POLICY
Product Specification Document
Version: 3.2 | Updated: 2025-01

VEHICLE MODEL: F150 (2024-2026 Model Years)

=== WARRANTY COVERAGE OVERVIEW ===

PRIMARY WARRANTY COVERAGE
Coverage Category: Comprehensive Factory Warranty
Coverage Amount: $7,500 maximum aggregate per vehicle per year
Duration: 3 years / 36,000 miles (whichever comes first)
Geographic Scope: Continental United States, Canada, Puerto Rico

WARRANTY EXCLUSIONS & LIMITATIONS

The following are NOT covered under Ford F150 warranty:

1. NORMAL WEAR & MAINTENANCE
   - Brake pads, rotors (first 15,000 miles covered, then customer responsibility)
   - Oil changes and filter replacement
   - Spark plugs and air filters
   - Tire wear (tires covered under separate Goodyear warranty)
   - Windshield wipers and washer fluid
   - Light bulbs and fuses

2. DAMAGE FROM MISUSE OR NEGLIGENCE
   - Towing beyond manufacturer specification (F150 capacity: 14,000 lbs)
   - Off-road use exceeding moderate trail conditions
   - Collision, accident, or impact damage
   - Water damage from submersion (wading depth limit: 24 inches)
   - Overloading cargo bed (payload capacity: 3,250 lbs)

3. ENVIRONMENTAL & EXTERNAL FACTORS
   - Rust or corrosion (except structural rust within 5 years)
   - Weather damage (hail, flood, hurricane)
   - Road salt and chemical exposure
   - UV damage to paint or interior

4. MODIFICATIONS & AFTERMARKET PARTS
   - Modifications not approved by Ford dealer
   - Non-OEM replacement parts
   - Suspension lift kits installed without factory approval
   - Engine tuning or ECU reprogramming

5. WEAR-OUT ITEMS (Covered only in first 12 months/12,000 miles)
   - Battery (typically 3-5 years typical, covered 12 months)
   - Water pump
   - Alternator
   - Starter motor

SPECIFIC COVERAGE DETAILS

Transmission Warranty:
   Amount: $2,500 maximum per repair
   Duration: 3 years / 36,000 miles
   Exclusion: Transmission failure due to low fluid (customer maintenance responsibility)

Engine Warranty:
   Amount: $3,200 maximum per major repair
   Duration: 5 years / 60,000 miles (extended for powertrain)
   Coverage: Manufacturing defects, valve train, gaskets
   Exclusion: Damage from improper fuel quality (must use Top Tier gasoline)

Electronics & Infotainment Warranty:
   Amount: $1,500 maximum
   Duration: 3 years / 36,000 miles
   Coverage: Radio, climate control, telematics unit, instrument cluster
   Exclusion: Damage from aftermarket installation or audio modifications

DEDUCTIBLE & CLAIM PROCESS
Deductible: $100 per claim
Claim Filing: Must file within 30 days of repair completion
Dealer Requirement: Must use Ford-authorized dealer for warranty service
Pre-Approval: Claims exceeding $500 require Ford Warranty Services pre-approval

=== WARRANTY CLAIM VALIDATION CRITERIA ===

To validate a warranty claim, the following conditions must be met:

1. Vehicle within warranty period (3 years / 36,000 miles)
2. Regular maintenance records verified (oil changes every 10,000 miles)
3. No service records from unauthorized facilities
4. Original owner or documented ownership transfer
5. No warranty exclusions applicable to claim
6. Mileage documentation consistent with recorded history

DEALER RESPONSIBILITIES
- Perform complete diagnostic prior to repair approval
- Document failure code and root cause analysis
- Submit warranty claim within 48 hours of customer authorization
- Provide itemized repair estimate
- Maintain F150-specific parts inventory

CUSTOMER RESPONSIBILITIES
- Maintain vehicle per schedule in owner's manual
- Report issues promptly (within 30 days of symptom onset)
- Provide proof of purchase and service history
- Allow authorized diagnostic inspection

=== REGIONAL WARRANTY VARIATIONS ===

United States: Standard 3 year / 36,000 mile coverage as specified
Canada: 3 years / unlimited mileage (more favorable than US terms)
Puerto Rico: 3 years / 36,000 miles (same as US)

Note for Dealers: Regional differences impact claim approval workflows and customer eligibility.

END OF F150 WARRANTY SPECIFICATION
